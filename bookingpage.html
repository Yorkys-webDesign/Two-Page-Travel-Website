  2. Punta Cana: Catamaran Boat to Saona Island with Buffet Lunch

    3. Benefit from hotel pickup and drop-off, stop off at a huge natural swimming pool,
    and enjoy snacks as you go. Experience the services of a professional guide,
    ride a speed boat and catamaran, and feast on a delicious buffet lunch including drinks.

    Begin your tour by boarding a speedboat and jetting off toward Saona Island.
    On the way, stop to swim in a crystal-clear natural swimming pool.
    Keep an eye out, if you're lucky, you'll see some beautiful starfish.

    When you arrive at Isla Saona, you'll have many activities to choose from.
    Wade in the turquoise waters, visit the open bar, or take a nap on a beach lounge chair.
    You can walk along the coastline or play a game of volleyball with your group.

    Indulge in a typical Dominican lunch of chicken, pork, rice, beans, salad, and fruit.
    You can even enjoy lobster and premium drinks when you book the VIP option.
    Take your time to discover the island and truly do it all.

  3. Monkeyland and Plantation Safari Tour from Punta Cana

    4. Exploring the Dominican Republic beyond the resort area can be challenging without a car.
    This half-day trip to Monkeyland makes it simple, with round-trip transportation from
    Bavaro and Punta Cana and a guided journey into the jungle. Visit local coffee and
    cacao plantations, learn about organic farming methods, and stroll through botanical gardens.

    Spend time observing squirrel monkeys in a natural setting and enjoy samples of fresh
    coffee or hot chocolate along the way. Venture beyond your resort without having to
    hire a vehicle or tackle the local roads. Prebooked entry to Monkey World ensures
    your spot inside this popular attraction with guaranteed hotel pickup and drop-off.
